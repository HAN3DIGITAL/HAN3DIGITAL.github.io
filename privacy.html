Cookies

Cookies and other similar technologies are used to cover a large amount of the User Information we collect and the advertising data we use. Cookies are a string of numbers and/or letters that a website saves on the User's hard disc. Pixel tags, internet storage, and other technologies that are similar to cookies will all be referred to as "cookies" in this section. Cookies enable platforms to "remember" you for the duration of your visit (session cookie) as well as for subsequent visits (persistent cookie).

Matchoholic uses the following type of cookies under the platform:

Mandatory Cookies - These cookies are essential for the User to browse the platform securely and to provide the Services that users specifically request.

Functionality Cookies - These cookies increase the functionality of the platform by storing User preferences. For example, these cookies can remember your name and location if you provide your name and location and the type of content you relate to.

Performance Cookies - These cookies improve the performance of the platforms. For example, these cookies help the page load faster.

Analytics Cookies - We may use third party analytics services such as Google Analytics and others. The User’s the IP address and other information; Will be collected automatically to evaluate your use of the website, collect reports on website activity, determine User behavior and provide related services. For more information on how Google Analytics uses your information, please click here. Additionally, authentication and track records will be used to collect User analytics.

Targeting / Advertisement - These cookies help us serve relevant ads and enforce continuity limit procedures.
